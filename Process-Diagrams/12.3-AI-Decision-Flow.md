# 12.3 AI Decision Flow

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Start: Customer Submits Documents] --> B[OCR & AI Extraction]

    B --> C{Are Documents Valid?}
    C -->|Yes| D[Check Aadhaar via UIDAI API]
    C -->|No| E[Reject: Invalid Documents]

    D --> F{Is Aadhaar Verified?}
    F -->|Yes| G[Check PAN via NSDL API]
    F -->|No| E

    G --> H{PAN Verified?}
    H -->|Yes| I[Run Fraud/AML Screening]
    H -->|No| E

    I --> J{Fraud Detected?}
    J -->|Yes| K[Escalate to Compliance]
    J -->|No| L[AI Auto-Approval]

    K --> M[Manual Review Decision]
    M -->|Approved| L
    M -->|Rejected| E

    L --> N[Customer Onboarded]
    E --> O[Process Ends]
